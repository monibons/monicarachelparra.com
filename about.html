Hello! I'm Monica Parra, a product designer living outside of NYC in Hoboken, NJ.

I began my design career as a digital designer, working at publishing companies such as Newsweek and SPIN, where my focus encompassed a blend of interactive storytelling and web design.

Following my time there, I pivoted to product design working with startups. In between startup ventures, I completed a design fellowship with Significance Labs, building products to assist lower-income families. I enjoyed a long tenure as a design leader at JW Player, working in video tech, followed by a role at Mayo Clinic leading design for clinical systems. I am currently at Lithic, leading design for data privacy and digital wallets.
